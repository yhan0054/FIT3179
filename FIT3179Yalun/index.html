Internet Users around the world in 2022 Report

Author: Yalun Han

Date: 16/10/2022

This data visualisation indicates the number of internet users in the world from 1980-2022, it shows the growth of the network through years, which also indicates the GDP that country owns in the specific year. Through this visualisation, you would also see the country with the most amount of people using network and the country with the least amount of the people using network.
